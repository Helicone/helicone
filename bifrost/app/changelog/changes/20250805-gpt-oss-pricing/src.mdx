Cost tracking is now available for GPT-OSS models across multiple providers.

## What's New

- **Fireworks Models**: `accounts/fireworks/models/gpt-oss-20b` and `accounts/fireworks/models/gpt-oss-120b`
- **Groq Models**: `openai/gpt-oss-20b` and `openai/gpt-oss-120b`
- **OpenRouter Models**: `openai/gpt-oss-20b` and `openai/gpt-oss-120b`
- **Automatic Detection**: Costs calculated automatically when using GPT-OSS models
- **Dashboard Integration**: View GPT-OSS usage alongside other models in your cost analytics

With this update, teams using GPT-OSS models can track their spending across Fireworks, Groq, and OpenRouter providers and compare costs with other AI models.
