### 🌏 In case you missed it: Open Stats

[image]

We publicly released all of our LLM usage statistics last week. 1.5B+ requests, 1108B+ tokens, 16TB of anonymized data, is now available. This is the world’s largest public AI conversation dataset, and we’re excited to see what the world can do with it. 

View live stats [link]. 


### Fireworks AI x Helicone

Fireworks AI helps developers run, fine-tune, and share large language models. We now have cost support and a dedicated domain for Fireworks! (fireworks.helicone.ai). Here's the doc [link]. 


### Dify x Helicone

Dify is an open-source LLM app development platform that lets you go from prototype to production quickly. We just partnered with Dify to add observability and logging there. Here's the doc [link]. 


### Bite-size updates 🍪

🍪 Sessions UI update. This week, we polished the Sessions UI and added a ‘Chat view’ to seamlessly follow your user’s AI conversation. 

🍪 Helicone Prompts Package. We are open-sourcing our prompts formatting package that fully supports chats. 

🍪 Demo baked into Helicone. Head over to Settings to enable a live mini game where you can demo Helicone and our latest features. 

🍪 Filter tree. Some of you have been asking for advance filtering. We just made it possible on the Request page. 

🍪 GitHub updates. We added a Contributors list (thanks y’all!), bug report & feature request templates and some home updates. 

🍪 Llama 3.1 support. Available in Together AI and Fireworks AI.
