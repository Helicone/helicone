Cost tracking is now available for xAI's Grok 4 model.

## What's New

- **Models**: `grok-4` and `x-ai/grok-4` (OpenRouter)
- **Automatic Detection**: Costs calculated automatically when using Grok models
- **Dashboard Integration**: View Grok 4 usage alongside other models in your cost analytics

With this update, teams using xAI's Grok models can track their spending and compare costs across different AI providers.
