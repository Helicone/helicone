### Feature updates

#### Full support for new models 

[image]

We just added full support for new Claude models, Gemini, Groq, Cohere and Mistral. Cost calculation just got easier. 


#### Together AI x Helicone Integration 

[image]

You can now run observability on Together with the most up-to-date pricing and stats - all with a custom domain: `together.hconeai.com/v1`. We're excited to partner with Together AI to bring openness and transparency to the future of AI. 

#### 🚀 Cloudflare Workers Launchpad

[image]

We are super excited to be selected as 1 of the 29 startups to be a part of Cloudflare's launchpad program. In just over a year, we've logged **1.5+ billion logs** and supported **800+ companies** daily in production—and we're just getting started! Check out the <a href="https://blog.cloudflare.com/2024-community-update" target="_blank">complete list of companies</a> in our cohort. 





### Bitesize updates 

🍪 **Moderation Integration.** Safeguard your chat completions by including `Helicone-Moderations-Enabled: true` for all OpenAI chat completion requests. 

🍪 **S3 Support.** No more payload size limitations such as embeddings, b64 images, large token counts. 

🍪 **Smart filters and other dashboard improvements.** Saved dashboard filters, better table columns and a new country chart 🌍. 

🍪 **Helm chart available now**. Currently for <a href="https://www.helicone.ai/pricing" target="_blank">Enterprise</a> and private access. 

🍪 **We made it easier for you to <a href="https://github.com/Helicone/helicone/tree/main/costs" target="_blank">contribute cost changes</a>.**
