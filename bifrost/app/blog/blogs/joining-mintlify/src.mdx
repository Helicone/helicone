Helicone has been acquired by Mintlify, and our team will be joining them in San Francisco.

**What this means for you:** Helicone's services will remain live for the foreseeable future in maintenance mode until we migrate our users to another platform. This means security updates, new models, bug & performance fixes all keep shipping.

Now for the story…

When we started Helicone three years ago during YC W23, everyone was building a "GPT wrapper." Every Twitter launch using GPT was going viral. Agents were in their infancy with AutoGPT. It was the first wave of AI-native products, and it was clear that observability for LLMs was going to become its own thing. We saw the gap and decided to go after it.

![YC W23 batch photo](/static/blog/yc-photo.png)

We were one of the first movers in the space. Back then, there wasn't really an "LLM observability market." We were building it as we went. Other startups in our YC batch started asking for access. Then they kept using it. Then they told their friends.

Three years later, our users have logged over 5.2 billion requests through Helicone. 16,000 organizations have trusted us with their LLM infrastructure. We've helped track over 33 million end users. We hit #1 on Product Hunt and became the most-used LLM observability platform by YC companies. **<span style={{color: '#0ea5e9'}}>That still blows our minds.</span>**

## Why Mintlify

Running Helicone, we've seen thousands of companies building with AI. We know what works and what doesn't. Mintlify has product-market fit in a way we've rarely seen.

We believe the cost of outdated knowledge will be massive in an agentic future. When agents act on behalf of users, where they pull from is critical. Mintlify is building that layer.

Han and Hahnbee are exceptional. Fast execution, deep focus, real attention to detail. They've built a team and culture we're excited to join. From day one it's been clear we fit.
