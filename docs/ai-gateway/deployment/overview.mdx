## Choosing the Right Option

- **NPX/Cargo**: Perfect for local development and testing
- **Docker**: Great for consistent deployments across environments
- **Kubernetes**: Ideal for production workloads requiring scaling and orchestration
- **Cloud Platforms**: Best for production deployments with managed infrastructure
- **Platform Services**: Excellent for rapid prototyping and simplified deployments

Need help deciding? Check out our [deployment comparison guide](/deployment/comparison) or reach out to our community for recommendations based on your specific use case.
