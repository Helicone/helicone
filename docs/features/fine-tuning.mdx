## Dataset Curation

With Helicone, you can easily create and manage high-quality datasets for fine-tuning:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Evaluate Requests**: Review your existing requests and assign scores based
    on quality and relevance.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Filter and Add**: Use Helicone's advanced filtering to select high-scoring
    requests and add them to your dataset.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Curate and Refine**: Modify the selected requests to create a perfect,
    high-quality dataset tailored to your needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fine-Tuning Integration

Helicone offers streamlined options for fine-tuning integration:

- **JSONL Export**: Easily export your curated datasets in JSONL format, compatible with various fine-tuning platforms.
- **OpenPipe Integration**: Connect directly to OpenPipe (openpipe.ai) to send your data and fine-tune open-source models seamlessly.
- **Iteration Support**: Quickly iterate on your datasets and fine-tuning processes based on real-world performance data captured by Helicone.

By leveraging Helicone's dataset curation and fine-tuning integration features, you can significantly improve your LLM's performance on specific tasks and domains.
